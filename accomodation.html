Offers a comfortable and convenient stay right in the heart of downtown Montréal. Ideally situated near popular local attractions, the hotel is just a short walk to McGill University. Guests will appreciate the spacious suites equipped with modern amenities, including complimentary Wi-Fi and a full-service kitchen. The hotel's prime location makes it easy to explore nearby attractions such as Mont Royal and the Montréal Museum of Fine Arts.

Located in the heart of Montréal, Delta Hotels by Marriott Montréal offers a comfortable and convenient stay.
